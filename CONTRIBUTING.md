### Steps to complete this task:

1. **Navigate to your repository:** Open your forked repository on GitHub.
2. **Create a new file:** Click the **Add file** button and select **Create new file**.
3. **Name the file:** Type `CONTRIBUTING.md` in the file name box.
4. **Add the content:** Paste the markdown text provided above into the edit box.
5. **Commit the changes:** Click the green **Commit changes...** button at the top right, and commit directly to the `main` branch.
6. **Get the URL:** Once saved, click on `CONTRIBUTING.md` from your repository's main page and copy the URL from your browser's address bar.
